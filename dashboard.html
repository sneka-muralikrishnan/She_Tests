    Health Tips

    Eat Iron-Rich Foods

    Boost your energy by eating iron-rich foods like spinach, beans, and lean meats during your cycle.

    Stay Hydrated

    Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and support overall well-being during your cycle.

    Herbal Teas for Relief

    Consider drinking herbal teas like chamomile to ease cramps and bloating during your period.

    Exercise Regularly

    Regular exercise, such as walking or yoga, can help alleviate period cramps and improve mood.

    Maintain a Balanced Diet

    Eat a well-balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains to maintain overall health.

    Get Enough Sleep

    Adequate sleep helps your body recover and manage hormonal changes during your cycle. Aim for 7-9 hours.
